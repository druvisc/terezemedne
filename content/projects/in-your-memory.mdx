Gifting flowers feels noble and reminiscent. In three months 5 monuments in the center of Riga are dated in photographs - Barclay de Tolle, Rainis, Savchenko, Pushkin and a scientist. Afterwards, the flower arrangements are reproduced in vacuum bags, symbolizing the transmission of traditions from generation to generation.
